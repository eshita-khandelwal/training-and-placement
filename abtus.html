ABOUT US


Training and Placement Cell in the Institute plays a vital role and is becoming a key department of the Institute.Training & Placement Cell is devoted to cater to the needs of organizations in conducting campus interviews for placements. It is headed by a Professor designated as Training & Placement Officer in charge. The Cell is assisted by Student Placement Coordinators who lead a team of placement representatives from various courses of study. The Training & Placement Cell ensures and takes care to provide the best arrangements and hospitality for the visiting companies' officials. As there are many more Engineering Colleges coming up , the competition for employment is increasing every day and the job of placement is becoming a challenging one.

Role of Training & Placement Cell

1) The Training & Placement Office of the Institute has the responsibility of facilitating the Campus Recruitment Programme for all disciplines.
2) The Placement Office initiates and nurtures contacts with leading organizations for campus visits and is functional throughout the year. It endeavors to match the aspirations of the students with the needs of the Industry. It also assists the students for Summer/Winter Industrial training.
3) The T&P Division also provides counseling and guidance to the students regarding the visiting organizations, selection procedure, preparation methods & resources thereby ensuring that the students are adequately equipped to face the recruitment tests.
4) A team of Student Placement Coordinators duly assists the T&P Office.
5) The auditorium, seminar halls and Interview cubicles, equipped with audio-visual aids provide the ideal venue for campus selection activities like Presentations, Written Test, Group Discussion and Interviews.
6) The Placement Office ensures and takes care to provide the best arrangement and hospitality for officials of the visiting companies.


Activities of Training and Placement Office

1) Arranging inplant training for students
2) Inviting various organizations for campus recruitment.
3) Developing verbal and non-verbal communications
4) Enhancing public speaking skills
5) Imparting personality improvement through in-house and external expert faculty Programme
6) Programs Providing Career planning and Career Mapping
7) Administering aptitude tests
8) Conducting group discussions and mock interview practice sessions
9) Arranging on and off campus recruitments
10) Personality development
11) Interpersonal & Communication skills

Placement Procedure

1) Invitations are sent to the Companies by the T&P office.
2) To the responding companies, the details of the final year students opting for placement are sent as per their requirement.
3) The companies have an option to go for the campus recruitment to the Constituent Colleges.
4) The company is assigned a convenient date.
5) After confirmation by the company, a pre-placement talk is arranged on the date.
6) Once the intimation is received from the companies they are displayed on the T&P notice board so that the interested students may submit their names.
7) The resumes of the students are then sent to the organization along with a tentative date for the campus interview (negotiable).
8) After confirmation the office arranges a Pre-Placement Talk.
9) The company conducts its selection and shortlists students for the interview.
10) The results are announced usually on the same day.
11) Students who receive confirmation letters are not allowed to appear before any other visiting company.
12) Once into the selection process, no candidate can drop out at an intermediate stage.

We have seen over the years that the students trained in this institute are very well prepared to handle responsibilities and work with the most cutting-edge technology in the industry. This belief works as a driving force for us to try and ensure that our students get a jump start in their career. We,at Training and Placement cell, provide our students with the best avenues to utilize their skills by helping them find gainful employment with the best of the engineering firms.
